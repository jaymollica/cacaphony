INSTRUCTIONS

This experience requires multiple devices, such as phone, tablet, laptop for a fuller, more orchestral experience. This should work across all platforms but we’ve found the best browsers to be Chrome and Firefox. If you are asked for your address, we will use this to then send you a postcard of a limited edition print, but only if you want one. Shake, click or touch your screen the experience will move with you. Move your smaller device to a dark place. Close your eyes and listen, open your eyes and watch. Move your devices next to each other. Open multiple tabs in a browser and stay with this experience for as long as you’d like.
